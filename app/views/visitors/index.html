How it Works

We realized that organising events can be a plainful experience, but it doesn’t have to be. That’s why our platform allows organisers to explore inspiring spaces and then confirm their bookings in few simple steps​

1. Find your ideal space

Browse and filter different spaces by your criteria (such as size, location, price range or type of event.)

2. Customize your event

Explore additional services such as catering, decoration or equipment to design your perfect event.​

3. Complete your booking

In few clicks you can book and pay your space securely.

About Us


  • Here is a random question: what could have a young parent, a corporate techie and a short-film festival lover might have in common?​


    The realization that although events are meant to connect us with friends, colleagues, family or like-minded people, they are surprisingly difficult to organise and tend to happen in non-original settings.​


    So we set out on a mission to change this – while also allow our community generates jobs, profit and business opportunities in order to reshape our society for brighter future.

Our Mission

Connect people and provide value back to community

Whether you want to launch a pop-up show, have an unused rooftop this week-end, love shoes (but not shoe boxes), cook amazing vegan buffets,... we want to enable all of you in your passions.

Open doors that normally would be closed​

By allowing private or re-purposed commercial spaces, we can all discover new locations. We want to foster innovative uses for spaces during off-peak times or when they would normally be unavailable.

Make your life a little bit easier​

We want to help organisers to book a event space hassle-free. We also allow providers to promote their services to a new audience. We coordinate in the background so you can enjoy more the fun parts.
